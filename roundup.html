Furnace Keeper Returns to Lower Gears
Henrik Marsh, formerly of the Ashgate Rail Works, returned to his post this week after a period of medical leave. His fellow workers say the headaches have not returned. The hall has been fitted with new ventilation.
Seamstress Guild Reports Uptick in Referrals
The Threadneedle Textile Works guild physician has begun routine blood checks for workers on extended shifts. The guild cites a recent case in which a long-serving embroiderer was found to have been working through a prolonged and undetected condition.
Fog Thickens Over the Quay — Third Week Running
Residents of the Quay district report persistent low fog since the new month began. The city engineers attribute the phenomenon to pressure differentials from the eastern furnace works. Ward Zero reports a slight increase in respiratory complaints.
